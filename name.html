What with the name?

University of Waterloo is in Canada. Our official motto is A Mari Usque Ad Mare (From Sea to Sea).

In Latin, e mari means “From the sea”. As we all are.

Both amari and emari are taken as github organization’s name, hence aemari.

Narrator: As an alumnus, Jakub eloquently suggested to change the name to “amarie”. Thanks man. I would like to keep my head where it is, which is on top of my neck.
