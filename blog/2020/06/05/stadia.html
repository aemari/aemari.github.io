Verifying Stadia under a less than ideal network condition.

June 05, 2020

Google Stadia is the future of gaming, according to Google Stadia. In order to make sure that Stadia’s customers can enjoy the service in as many network conditions as possible, our Alumnus, Jakub Kuderski, who also happened to be a Stadia engineer, downgraded his network connection from 1Gbps to 100Mbps. He said “the weekend started fantastic, boys”.
